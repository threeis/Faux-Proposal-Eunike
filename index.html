WHAT WE RECOMMEND

Branding Consulting Service

We will undergo these 6 phases with you and develop a unique solution according to your specific needs:

  1. Discover - gaining a clear and comprehensive insight into your business and its main objectives. In discovery phase, we discover in detail about your target audience(s), short and long term marketing and sales objectives and key competitors. We also invite discussion of your businessâ€™ values and your individual visual preferences. 
  2. Ideation - generating solutions to your need. Once we feel confident that we have fully understood the brief, we begin the idea-generation phase, which may include brainstorming with our project team and discuss every possibility before finally reducing the possibilities into a few most valuable ideas which we will develop further.
  3. Presentation - presenting the proposed visual identity to you, along with proposed social media channels and languages used in communicating with clients and prospects (ex: formal, casual, witty, etc). We will present 3 concepts of logo design with the accompanying color palettes, along with the underlying reasons behind their creation. 
  4. Feedback / revisions - listening to your feedback and revising the initial concept until it reaches both your satisfaction and our ideals. In this stage, you can let us know what you think about our proposed solutions, and we will tweak them. You will have 3 chances of free revisions, and any further revisions will be quoted per hour.
  5. Execution - getting the approved visual identity and other branding elements to life. We will provide you the full logo set and business card, build your website according to the branding, set up your social media channels and administer them.
